My Cooking Journey

I have been a collector of cookbooks for many years; I read them like they were novels. Turning the pages to discover new and exciting dishes.

When my mother passed away unexpectedly my father asked me what of my mother's did I want and before he could finish the question I answered, 'her cookbook'.

Before there was ever a Food Network channel I was watching cooking shows on PBS; Frugal Gourmet, Martha Stewart, Julia Childs to name a few.
